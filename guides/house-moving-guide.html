In short

A whole-house move has more moving parts than people expect: every room, the garage, the attic, the patio, and the shed all have to be packed, loaded, and put back in order. The trick is to work room by room over several weeks instead of attacking the whole house in one panicked weekend.

This guide lays out the order that works, what a house move costs around Bradenton and Sarasota, and the Florida details that matter, like moving in summer heat and timing around hurricane season.

Start with the rooms you barely use

Pack the garage, guest rooms, closets, and storage first. These hold the most rarely-touched items, and packing them early gives you an honest sense of how much you actually own, which is what a mover needs to size the truck and crew.

Sort as you pack

A move is the cheapest time to get rid of things, because you pay to move every box. Make three piles in each room: keep, donate, and toss. Manatee and Sarasota County both have donation centers that will take furniture in good condition.

Kitchens take longer than you think

The kitchen is usually the slowest room because of fragile and oddly shaped items. Use dish-pack boxes with dividers for glassware, wrap each piece, and pack plates on their edges rather than flat. Leave out only what you need for the last few days.

Furniture, beds, and big items

Beds, tables, and large furniture get disassembled, wrapped in moving blankets, and labeled with their hardware bagged and taped to the piece. A good crew handles this on the day, but knowing what comes apart helps you plan the loading order.

What a house move costs here

Local house moves are usually quoted by the size of the home and the crew and truck needed, not a vague hourly guess. A two-bedroom is a different job than a five-bedroom with a pool table and a garage full of tools. Get a walkthrough so the quote matches the real house.

Florida timing

Start early in the day from late spring through summer to beat the heat and afternoon storms. If you are moving during hurricane season, watch the forecast and keep a backup date in mind. Keep water and a fan handy for everyone working.

Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to move a house?expand_more

A typical three-bedroom local move runs most of a day with a full crew. Larger homes or long carries can run longer. We give you a realistic window with your quote.

Should I pack myself or hire packing?expand_more

Either works. Packing yourself saves money if you start early. Full or partial packing saves time and is worth it for fragile kitchens and busy households. Many people split it: pack the easy rooms, let us handle the kitchen and breakables.

Do you move everything, including the garage and patio?expand_more

Yes. Tell us about the garage, shed, patio furniture, and any heavy or specialty items at the estimate so the crew and truck are sized correctly.
